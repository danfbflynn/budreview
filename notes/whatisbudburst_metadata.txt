datasetID:	study ID name

respvar.simple:	response variables used in study

Percent:	definition of budburst based on percent budburst of individual (i.e., 1% means that only 1% of individual needs to have bursted bud in order to have counted for that particular study)

Definition:	how the study defines budburst 

Flowerburst?: Yes/No if they looked at flower buds instead of or in addition to leaf buds. Yes for flower buds, no for only leaf buds

Scale: if the study defined budburst using a particular scale (e.g., BBCH scale) - sometimes the author made new scale, which will have corresponding notes in the 'notes' column

Notes: extra notes about the study
