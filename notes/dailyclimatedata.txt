To date (June 2017) we have used:

Livneh dataset for North America (see below) with data from 1955-2013
E-OBS for Europe: http://www.ecad.eu/download/ensembles/download.php (as of June 2017)

We pulled climate data in July 2016 and continued working on through 2017 to calculate chilling (and possibly more).
